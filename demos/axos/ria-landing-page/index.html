Axos Advisor Services Redesign

Prepared for Axos Bank by DGTL Sunrise. A best-in-class conversion rate optimization (CRO) redesign focusing on improving Google & Meta ad traffic conversion rate performance.

Launch RIA Landing Page

Executive Summary

This redesign transforms the Axos landing page into a high-converting, mobile-optimized acquisition funnel. By addressing cognitive load, form positioning, and mobile friction, these optimizations are structured to maximize qualified advisor leads.

Redesign Key Improvements & UX Rationale

01

Empathy-Driven Copywriting & Framing

Original Layout Sparse, generic text blocks that focused purely on a download list rather than speaking to the advisor's core obstacles.
Optimized Redesign Direct copy addressing "the transition excuse" and aligning value benefits with paid search/social intent.
Conversion Rate Impact: Highest (Primary Persuasion Driver)
Addressing the advisor's fear of transition builds instant affinity. Framing the switch as structured and simple builds credibility, lowering barriers to download the guide.
02

Above-the-Fold Form Integration

Original Layout Form was located at the very bottom of the page, requiring extensive scrolling or anchor navigation.
Optimized Redesign Split-screen desktop hero containing the lead capture card directly visible upon page load.
Conversion Rate Impact: Highest (Primary Structural Driver)
Paid ad traffic has extremely brief attention spans. Presenting the lead form immediately alongside the value proposition eliminates scrolling friction and captures high-intent leads instantly.
03

Mobile-First Layout & Floating CTA

Original Layout Cramped layouts on mobile device sizes with crowded headers and buried CTA buttons.
Optimized Redesign Responsive stacked single-column layout, mobile header styling, and floating scroll-driven CTA button.
Conversion Rate Impact: High
A significant portion of paid traffic is mobile. A clean stacked layout with a floating CTA ensures easy form access at any scroll depth.
04

High-Visibility Direct Contact Access

Original Layout No direct phone number or contact information was present in the header of the original page (only buried in the footer).
Optimized Redesign Prominent header phone link with advisor consultation hours on desktop and a tap-to-call mobile shortcut pill.
Conversion Rate Impact: High / Trust Builder
Many high-value advisors prefer immediate direct communication. Providing a clear phone channel builds credibility and captures inbound hot-leads who skip the lead form. Call-Tracking Recommendation: Implement Dynamic Number Insertion (DNI) via a call-tracking partner (e.g. CallRail) to capture calls as conversion events in Google Ads and Meta Pixels, ensuring accurate CPA attribution and campaign optimization.
05

Interactive Red Flags & Curiosity Loop

Original Layout The 5 warning signs were completely absent from the landing page body, providing zero preview of the guide's content.
Optimized Redesign Interactive desktop tab switcher (accordion on mobile) presenting warning signs dynamically with core stats.
Conversion Rate Impact: High
An interactive tab/accordion increases dwell time. Teasing the warning signs with data (e.g. 92% switching because of tech) builds strong curiosity and establishes the guide's value.
06

Trust Signals & Credibility Ribbon

Original Layout No regulatory membership badges (FINRA/SIPC) or institutional trust markers presented in the page body (only standard legal footer disclaimers existed).
Optimized Redesign Dedicated trust bar positioned immediately below the hero highlighting credentials and integrated banking benefits.
Brand Perception Impact: Highest
Financial advisors are highly risk-averse when sharing credentials. Displaying regulatory memberships (FINRA/SIPC) and custody backing immediately establishes authority and safety.
07

Lead Capture Form Visual Redesign & UX Polish

Original Layout A plain, basic form hidden at the bottom of the page, lacking visual structure, autofill attributes, or mobile keyboard helper settings.
Optimized Redesign A modern, high-contrast form card floating above the fold on a navy background, featuring inline validation, standard autofills, and auto-opening mobile dialpads.
Conversion Rate Impact: High
A form must look premium and trustworthy to convert. Combining a distinct visual card with mobile dialpads (which automatically open the number pad for the phone field) significantly reduces friction and raises conversion rates.
