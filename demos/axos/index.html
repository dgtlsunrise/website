Regulatory compliance notes

7 of 10 issues addressed directly in the demo pages. The remaining 3 items require data or regulatory review that only Axos's legal and compliance team can complete. See details below. This assessment is based on Regulation DD (Truth in Savings Act, 12 CFR Part 1030), the FTC Act Section 5(a) / UDAAP prohibition on unfair or deceptive acts and practices, and FDIC advertising rules (12 CFR Part 328). This is a design strategy document — it does not constitute legal advice and is not a substitute for review by qualified legal counsel.
Addressed in demo
Reg DD §1030.8(b) — "Annual percentage yield" spelled out
Both pages now spell out "annual percentage yield (APY*)" in full on the first hero mention before using the abbreviation, satisfying the Regulation DD requirement. The footer footnote on both pages also uses the full term.
Addressed in demo
Reg DD §1030.8(c)(1) — "Rate may change after account is opened"
Both pages now include an explicit "Rate may change after the account is opened" statement. On the Best In Class PDP this appears directly beneath the animated rate counter. On the Old PDP it appears below the hero CTA and in the strengthened footer footnote. Axos legal should confirm placement meets the "clearly and conspicuously" standard on mobile.
Addressed in demo
Reg DD §1030.8(c)(6) — "Fees may reduce earnings" footer footnote
Both pages carry "Fees may reduce earnings" in strengthened footer footnotes. The Best In Class PDP footer now reads: "Rate may change after the account is opened, as often as daily, without prior notice. Fees may reduce earnings." Axos legal should confirm final footnote sizing and placement meets the conspicuousness standard.
Addressed in demo
UDAAP — Competitor rate data flagged as illustrative
All competitor rate footnotes on both pages now include explicit language: "illustrative — verify against current published rates before publishing." The comparison table note on the Best In Class PDP similarly flags competitor rates as illustrative. Axos's team must update all competitor rates and the national average figure to current data before any page goes live.
Addressed in demo
UDAAP — Testimonials marked as demo placeholders
The Best In Class PDP testimonials section now carries a visible amber compliance notice: "Reviews below are illustrative placeholders for design purposes only. Replace with verified, documented real customer reviews before publishing." This notice is visible to anyone reviewing the page.
Addressed in demo
UDAAP — Application time claims qualified
Time claims on the Best In Class PDP now read "under 3 minutes for most applicants" rather than an unqualified absolute. Axos should validate this against real application completion data before publishing and adjust the specific time estimates as needed.
Addressed in demo
UDAAP — Trust bar stats marked for verification
A small note now appears beneath the trust bar on the Best In Class PDP: "Stats subject to verification prior to publishing — Demo document only." Before publishing, Axos should verify and update: 1M+ customer count, 4.8★ App Store rating, A+ BBB rating.
Still requires review
Reg DD §1030.8(c)(4) — Minimum balance disclosure placement
"No minimum balance" appears in body copy and footnotes on both pages. Axos legal should confirm that placement relative to each APY figure meets the "clearly and conspicuously" standard under Regulation DD — particularly on mobile where footnote text may be small.
Still requires review
FDIC Advertising Rule (12 CFR Part 328) — Updated 2025 digital sign requirements
Both pages carry FDIC language, but Axos legal should confirm exact wording and placement meets the updated FDIC digital advertising sign rules effective 2025. This cannot be verified from a design demo — it requires review against the specific regulatory text by qualified compliance staff.
Still requires review
UDAAP — "9x the national average" claim requires current data
The "9x national average" claim references FDIC data from 12/10/25. This figure must be recalculated against current FDIC national average data before publishing. If the current national average has changed, the multiplier must be updated or removed.
Compliant as designed
No "free" account claims triggering Reg DD
Neither page uses "free" to describe the account itself. "No fees" and "it's free" reference the cost to apply. Axos legal should confirm this usage is acceptable under Reg DD's "free" account advertising restrictions.
Compliant as designed
InsureGuard+ / IntraFi® attribution — accurate and attributed
The Best In Class PDP references InsureGuard+ Savings through IntraFi® Network Deposits℠ with the $265M figure and registered trademark, matching language on the live Axos page. This claim appears accurate and properly attributed.
